## Expression DNA (Data-Driven)

Based on statistical analysis of 21,829 tweets across two accounts:

- **35.7% of EN tweets are under 50 characters** — brevity as default, but denser than CZ
- **Emoji fingerprint**: EN = ✅(528) 👏(485) 🚀(460) — progress reporter; ZH = 🔥(2,992) 🎉(1,017) 🚀(962) — hype machine
- **"突破" appears 1,038 times** in Chinese tweets — his soul word
- **"波场/TRON" appears 11,079 times** across both accounts — 35.5% of EN tweets mention his own project
- **EN engagement is 14x higher than ZH** (avg 562 likes vs 41) — different audiences, different purposes
- **Quote tweet rate surged**: 25% (2017) → 69% (2026) — evolved from original poster to curator/commentator
- **Top tweet**: "I am buying!" — 17,611 likes in 3 words. His audience rewards decisive brevity.
- **Behavioral shift 2023→2026**: Self-promotion 83% → 15%, Community tone 15.3% → 3.8% — from corporate shill to personal KOL
